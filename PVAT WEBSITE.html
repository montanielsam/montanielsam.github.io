OUR ACHIEVEMENTS





2024 Asian Senior Beach Volleyball Championships

The Pilipinas Volleyball Association of Trainors, Inc. (PVAT) is proud to be one of the key groups deployed by the Philippine National Volleyball Federation (PNVF) for the 2024 Asian Senior Beach Volleyball Championships. As part of our contribution to the success of this prestigious competition, PVAT is providing highly skilled and dedicated personnel to serve as referees, line judges, scorers, and court managers throughout the event. We are honored to support the growth of beach volleyball and to help ensure the smooth and fair conduct of the championship.





Batang Pinoy 2024 at Puerto Princesa, Palawan

The Pilipinas Volleyball Association of Trainors (PVAT) is honored to have contributed to the success of the 2024 Batang Pinoy National Championships in Puerto Princesa, Palawan, through our role in officiating. We are proud to support the development of young athletes and ensure fair play throughout the competition. Our participation reflects our commitment to fostering excellence in volleyball and promoting grassroots sports in the Philippines. We thank the Philippine Sports Commission, the Puerto Princesa City Government, and all organizers for the opportunity, and we look forward to future collaborations.





Volleyball World Beach Pro Tour Challenge

The Pilipinas Volleyball Association of Trainors is honored to have been a part of the National Technical Officials for the Beach Pro Tour Challenge in Nuvali, Sta. Rosa, Philippines. As we reflect on the successful conclusion of the competition, we take pride in our role in ensuring the event was conducted with the highest standards of officiating. Our commitment to the growth of volleyball and to fostering a professional environment for athletes was evident throughout the event, and we look forward to continuing our support for the sport in future endeavors.

PNVF U21 Volleyball Championship

The Pilipinas Volleyball Association of Trainors is privileged to have served as officials for the PNVF U21 Volleyball Championships. As the competition concluded, we are proud of our contributions to ensuring the event ran smoothly and with integrity. Our commitment to the development of young athletes and to maintaining the highest standards of officiating played a key role in the success of this prestigious tournament. We remain dedicated to supporting the future of volleyball and look forward to continuing our involvement in nurturing the next generation of players and officials.
